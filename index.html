About

Meet Caroline Ackel

Caroline Ackel is a licensed 3rd generation professional auctioneer dedicated to making your fundraising event a tremendous success. With almost a decade of experience and a passion for helping charities reach their goals, she brings energy, expertise, and enthusiasm to every event.

Her dynamic auction style keeps guests engaged and excited, resulting in record-breaking fundraising results. Caroline has helped raise millions of dollars for worthy causes throughout the Texas, Louisiana, and Oklahoma.

The Difference

Why a Professional Auctioneer?

A skilled auctioneer doesn't just call bids — they command the room, build excitement, and keep donors engaged from the first item to the last. Studies show that professional auctioneers consistently raise 20–30% more than volunteer-led events, turning a good fundraiser into a great one.
